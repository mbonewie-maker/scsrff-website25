Our Mission

The Southern Cameroon Spiritual and Relief Friendship Foundation (S.C.S.R.F.F.) is dedicated to supporting internally displaced persons in Akwaya subdivision, Cameroon. Our mission extends beyond immediate relief - we believe in empowering communities through education and sustainable support.

Every donation directly impacts a child's ability to learn, grow, and build a better future. Together, we can transform lives through the power of education and community support.

Current Crisis

200+ families displaced from their homes
Countless children without access to education
Educational resources desperately needed

Back to School Initiative

Ensuring that education continues even in the most challenging circumstances

School Supplies

Providing essential educational materials including books, notebooks, pencils, and learning resources for displaced children.

Teacher Support

Supporting dedicated teachers who continue to educate children despite the challenging circumstances and limited resources.

Community Support

Building sustainable educational programs that empower entire communities and create lasting positive change.
